A Gut Health Overview



What are the consequences of prolonged gut inflammation?

Over a span of time, an accumulation of harmful bacteria in your gut initiates a process
where the protective barrier between your inner organs and bloodstream,
known as the gut lining, gradually erodes. As these breaches in the lining widen,
toxins and undigested food particles find their way into your bloodstream, giving rise
to a variety of persistent digestive problems.
